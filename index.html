Tired of all the bells and whistles that come complete in today’s phones? Hi-res cameras, speedy processors and cool apps just aren’t your thing? Whatever the case may be for you (or perhaps parents or grandparents), Phonotto is a wonderful breath of fresh air, it allows you to make/receive calls and send/receive SMS: no frills and no unnecessary features.

Phonotto is an Android app available on the Google Play Store, which means that it replaces your regular home screen with one specifically tailored for people who are just after call and text capabilities. Phonotto does away with the endless junk that many people have on their home screen, providing a very minimal alternative.

The new version require lot of development, the roadmap is hard and the new features are amazing!
